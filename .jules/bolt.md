## 2024-05-24 - Expensive DateFormatter Initialization in UITableViewCells
**Learning:** In iOS Swift development, `DateFormatter` is a notorious performance bottleneck when instantiated frequently. In `Sources/PhantomSwift/Modules/Logger/UI/LogConsoleVC.swift` and `Sources/PhantomSwift/Modules/Performance/UI/HangListVC.swift`, a new `DateFormatter` was created in the `configure(with:)` method of table view cells, which runs repeatedly during scroll, causing severe frame drops.
**Action:** When working on iOS codebases, always review cell `configure` methods or fast path iterations for `DateFormatter`, `NumberFormatter`, or other expensive localized/calendar-based Swift initializers. Refactor them into `static let` properties so they are initialized once and shared across instances.
